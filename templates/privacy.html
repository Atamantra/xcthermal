Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 6, 2025

Welcome to XcThermal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use our web application and services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your username, email address, and a hashed password.
  • User Content: We collect settings preferences (e.g., units, language, map style) and any feedback you provide.
  • Communications: If you contact us via email, we may collect your name, email address, and the content of your message.

1.2. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Location Data: To provide our core thermal map and weather analysis services, we collect and process geolocation data (latitude, longitude, and altitude) when you interact with the map or use the "Locate Me" feature.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with the Service, such as the features you use, the frequency of your visits, and your IP address.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device and browser you use to access the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service (e.g., generating thermal maps and AI weather interpretations).
  • To personalize your experience, such as saving your map preferences and flight settings.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account, updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Service.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activities.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share data with third-party vendors who help us operate the Service, including:
    • Meteoblue & Open-Meteo: For weather data and meteograms (Location coordinates are sent to these APIs).
    • Google Gemini (AI): For generating weather interpretations (Location and weather data summaries are processed by this AI).
    • Mapbox: For map tiles and geocoding services.
    • Brevo (formerly Sendinblue): For sending transactional emails (e.g., password resets, reports).
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business to another company.

4. Data Retention and Security

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: The right to request copies of your personal data.
  • Rectification: The right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • Deletion: The right to request that we erase your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten"), subject to certain conditions.
  • Restriction of Processing: The right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: The right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@xcthermal.com.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service (e.g., staying logged in).

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from Children. If you become aware that a Child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from Children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
